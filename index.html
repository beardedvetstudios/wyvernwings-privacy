Wyvern Wings — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Overview

Wyvern Wings is developed and published by [Steven Tuckett / Bearded Vet Studios]. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information is and is not collected when you play Wyvern Wings.

Data Collection

Wyvern Wings does not collect any personal data. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Payment information
  • Any other personal information

Local Save Data

Wyvern Wings saves your game progress (coins, level, unlocked stages, settings, and achievements) locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Third-Party Services

Wyvern Wings does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The game operates entirely offline and makes no network connections of any kind.

Children's Privacy

Wyvern Wings does not collect data from any users, including children. The game is rated for ages 13 and up.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

🛡️ Short version: We don't collect your personal data. Wyvern Wings saves your game progress locally on your device only. No accounts, no tracking, no ads.

Who We Are

Bearded Vet Studios is an independent game development studio founded by a military veteran. We develop and publish mobile games for iOS and Android.

Contact: beardedvetstudios@gmail.com

Information We Collect

Bearded Vet Studios does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from users of our games.

Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or contact information
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Any financial or payment information
  • Photos, contacts, or other device content

Game Save Data

Wyvern Wings saves your game progress (stages cleared, coins, unlocked items, settings) locally on your device only. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to our servers
  • Is not accessible to us or any third party
  • Is stored in encrypted format on your device

Third-Party Services

Our games do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not use:

  • Google Analytics or Firebase
  • Facebook SDK or Meta Pixel
  • Any advertising networks
  • Crash reporting services that collect personal data

App store platforms (Apple App Store, Google Play Store) may collect their own data when you download or purchase our apps. Please refer to Apple's and Google's respective privacy policies for information about their data practices.

In-App Purchases

Wyvern Wings is a paid application with no in-app purchases. All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). Bearded Vet Studios never receives, processes, or stores any payment or financial information.

Children's Privacy

Our games do not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since we collect no personal data from any user, our games are safe for players of all ages from a data privacy standpoint.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Since we do not collect contact information, we cannot notify users directly of changes — please check this page periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:
